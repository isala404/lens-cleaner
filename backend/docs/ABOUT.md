## Why TopPics?

Managing thousands of photos is overwhelming. Storage space runs out, and finding that one perfect shot in a burst of twenty takes forever. We built TopPics to solve this.

*   **100% Local Analysis**: We use WebAssembly to run powerful AI models directly on your device. Your photos stay private.
*   **Smart Grouping**: Our AI "sees" your photos like a human does, grouping similar shots based on content, not just timestamps.
*   **You Are In Control**: We never delete automatically. You review every suggestion.
